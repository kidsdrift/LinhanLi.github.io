Pallas And Collie



  • My original cartoon characters.
  • Initially a set of chat stickers on WeChat to express commen emotions in online conversations.
  • 2D/3D forms were used to depict the world they live in.
  • Are used in various virtual items.

Characters

Pallas is a small wild cat that lives in the snowfields and desert.
She has the usual disdainful expression of a manul. However, the fur, ears, and limbs were changed significantly to give the impression of a free and lazy adolescent.

Collie is a Border Collie. She has innocent eyes and a black-and-white coat.
Her charm is her innate contentment and sincerity.

They live in a flat in an otherworldly space, communicating with the outside world through a portal.

Stickers

Stickers created with pallas and collie have been used over 30,000 times in China's leading chat apps.
