Assassin's Creed Codename Jade

Assasin's Creed Codename Jade

An open-world AC Game for mobile devices. The game is set in the Qin Dynasty, where players embark on a journey to explore the vibrant world and culture of ancient China.

My responsibilities included

  • assembling ancient architectures.
  • designing expandable behavior to characters such as playing the Chinese zither and chess.
  • optimizing climbing and parkour capabilities for the protagonist.
  • designing and developing automated testing system in open world.
